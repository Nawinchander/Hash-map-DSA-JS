LRU Cache (HashMap + Doubly Linked List)
Idea

LRU = Least Recently Used

When cache is full:

Remove the least recently used item

To make operations O(1):

HashMap → fast lookup

Doubly Linked List → track usage order

Most recent → Head
Least recent → Tail
